The Best Movies I've Ever Seen

Top three movies of all time according to Jared Kasowski

The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

The epic conclusion to Tolkien's tale of Frodo destroying the One Ring.
I highly recommend watching the extended addition to get the full experience.

La La Land

Usually musicals are made for broadway and adapted to the screen. But this musical was born for the screen.
Make sure to bring a box of tissues. This fantastic film is a tear jerker.

The Mummy

Action, adventure, horror and comedy. What more could you ask for?
This movie has it all.
